The Young Earth Creationism


    The Earth and its lifeforms were created in their present forms by supernatural acts of the God of Abraham between approximately 6,000 and 10,000 years ago. In its most widespread version, YEC is based on the religious belief in the inerrancy of certain literal interpretations of the Book of Genesis.Its primary adherents are Christians and Jews who believe that God created the Earth in six literal days
